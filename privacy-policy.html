Privacy Policy

Welcome to Wellness General Hospital. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal health information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from you:

  • Personal identification information (name, date of birth, address, etc.)
  • Medical history and health information
  • Insurance and payment details
  • Contact information (phone number, email address, etc.)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and manage your healthcare services
  • Process billing and insurance claims
  • Contact you with important updates and reminders
  • Improve our services and patient experience

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal obligations
  • With other healthcare providers involved in your treatment
  • With your consent or at your request

Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact our Privacy Officer using the contact information below.
